## About This Viz

This piece follows the before-and-after statistical journeys of all seven seniors in the Class of 2026 each of whom arrived at Ole Miss through the transfer portal. The data tells a story of development, transformation, and what Coach Yo has built in Oxford.

The visualization is structured in three acts:

- **Act I – The Portal Effect** — Points per game and field goal percentage, before and after the transfer. Christeen Iwuala went from 3.3 PPG at UCLA to 15.0 PPG at Ole Miss. That's not development. That's transformation.
- **Act II – The Full Picture** — Small multiples across all four major stat categories (points, rebounds, assists, FG%) for all seven seniors, plus each player's scoring compared to the SEC positional average (10.8 PPG).
- **Act III – The Argument** — A composite Portal Impact Score ranking, and a breakdown of how much of Ole Miss's total offensive production this senior class accounts for.
