Act I

The Portal Effect

When Christeen Iwuala arrived at Ole Miss from UCLA, she had averaged 3.3 points per game over two seasons with the Bruins. She had two career starts. She was not a household name. This season she is averaging 15.0 points and 8.5 rebounds per game and has recorded multiple double-doubles. That is not development. That is transformation.
